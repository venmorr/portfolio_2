A bit about me...

Hello!

I'm Steven Morrison, a fullstack software developer with a creative mind and a passion for bringing ideas to life. I love coding because of the endless possibility it puts at my finger tips. Not only a powerful tool for creation, coding has the potential to shape the future.

Having spent the last eight years in a professional educational environment, I’ve established leadership traits, excelled in a highly collaborative atmosphere, and developed a keen attention to detail. These experiences have not only enriched my understanding of teamwork, but have also instilled in me the importance of precision in every aspect of my work.

What sets me apart is the unique lens through which I view the world. Living with dyslexia has not only presented challenges for me to gain mastery over but has also granted me advanced non-linear thinking allowing me to view things on a wider scale and make possibility many others sometimes miss. This distinct perspective fuels my creativity and enables me to uncover solutions to problems that others might not see.

Join me on a journey where creativity meets functionality, and let's build something extraordinary together.
