Conway's game of life

Hello, I'm Steven Morrison, a creative mind with a passion for bringing ideas to life through software development. My journey into the world of coding began as a pursuit of a powerful tool for creation, and I quickly realized it was my best avenue for shaping the future.

What sets me apart is the unique lens through which I view the world. Living with dyslexia has not only presented challenges but has also granted me the ability to think non-linearly. This distinctive perspective fuels my creativity and enables me to uncover solutions to problems that others might not see.

Having spent the last 8 years in the field of education, I've honed my skills in working collaboratively and developed a keen attention to detail. These experiences have not only enriched my understanding of teamwork but have also instilled in me the importance of precision in every aspect of my work.

Join me on a journey where creativity meets functionality, and let's build something extraordinary together.
